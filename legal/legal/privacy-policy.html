Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At GreenwayPost, we understand that handling your business and personal correspondence requires the highest level of trust. This Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and fiercely protect your information. We do not, and will never, sell your personal data or mail contents to third-party marketers or data brokers.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA) services in compliance with federal law, we collect:

  • Identity Data: Government-issued ID details required to notarize USPS Form 1583.
  • Account Data: Names, business names, contact information, and billing details.
  • Mail Data: Digital scans of the exterior of your mail and, only upon your explicit request, scans of internal documents.

3. Data Security and Encryption

Your security is our highest operational priority. All digital mail scans, dashboard interactions, and stored documents are secured using industry-standard 256-bit AES encryption. Access to our physical facility at 13009 Greenway Chase Ct is strictly limited to background-checked GreenwayPost personnel.

4. Information Sharing

We only share your information under the following limited circumstances:

  • With the USPS: We are legally required to file your Form 1583 with the United States Postal Service.
  • For Legal Compliance: We will disclose information if compelled by a valid court order, subpoena, or to cooperate with federal law enforcement regarding suspected mail fraud.
  • Service Providers: We use trusted third-party processors (e.g., Stripe for payments) who are bound by strict confidentiality agreements to facilitate our services.

5. Cookie Policy

We use essential cookies to maintain your login session and secure your digital dashboard. We also use non-essential analytics cookies to improve our website experience. You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the consent banner on our homepage.
