2. Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA) Regulations

GreenwayPost operates as a CMRA under the regulations of the United States Postal Service (USPS). To utilize our virtual address services, all clients must:

  • Complete, notarize, and submit USPS Form 1583.
  • Provide two valid forms of identification as mandated by federal law.
  • Authorize GreenwayPost to receive, open (upon explicit request), and forward mail on their behalf.

Failure to complete Form 1583 within the designated timeframe will result in the suspension of mail services and the return of incoming mail to the sender.
