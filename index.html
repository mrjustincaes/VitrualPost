Step 2

We Receive & Digitize

When mail or packages arrive from USPS, FedEx, UPS, or DHL, our local Houston staff logs the item. We securely photograph the exterior of the envelope or box and immediately send a discreet notification to your smartphone or email.

Step 3

You Take Command

Log into your secure digital dashboard to view your new mail. With a single tap, you can instruct us to:

  • 📄 Open & Scan: We scan the interior documents as a PDF.
  • 📦 Forward: Ship the physical item anywhere in the world.
  • ♻️ Shred & Recycle: Securely destroy unwanted mail.
  • 🏦 Deposit Checks: We mail your checks directly to your bank.
