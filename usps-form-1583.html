USPS Form 1583 Compliance

As a Commercial Mail Receiving Agency (CMRA) operating in the United States, GreenwayPost is required by the United States Postal Service to collect a notarized USPS Form 1583 from all clients before we can legally receive, open, or forward mail on your behalf.

What is Form 1583?

This federal form authorizes GreenwayPost to act as your mail receiving agent. It protects you from mail fraud and ensures that your identity is verified.

How to Complete the Form

  • Download the Form: Upon selecting a tier, you will receive a pre-filled PDF of Form 1583.
  • Provide Identification: You must present two forms of acceptable ID (e.g., Driver's License, Passport, Voter Registration).
  • Notarize: The form must be signed in the presence of a licensed notary public. GreenwayPost partners with online notary services to allow you to complete this step entirely via webcam in less than 10 minutes.
